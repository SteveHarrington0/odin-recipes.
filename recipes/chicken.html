Chicken

Description

Roasted chicken is very tasty and its a favourite dish for many nonveg lovers

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • Chilli powder
  • salt
  • coriander
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Wash the chicken with water
  2. Apply the Ginger garlic paste along with Chilli powder and Salt to the chicken
  3. Take a pan and put it on the stove
  4. Add required oil in the pan
  5. Then place the chicken pieces on the pan and roast
  6. Chicken recipe is ready
